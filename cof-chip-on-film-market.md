<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Double Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>COF (Chip-On-Film) technology is used in the manufacturing of flexible displays and electronic devices. In single layer COF market type, a single layer of integrated circuit chips is mounted on a thin film, reducing the overall size and weight of the device. In double layer COF market type, two layers of integrated circuit chips are stacked on top of each other, increasing the functionality and processing power of the device. Both single layer and double layer COF technologies offer compact and efficient solutions for electronic devices.</p></p>
