<p><p>Household ventilator market types include Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP), which delivers a constant level of air pressure to keep the airways open during sleep. Auto Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (Auto CPAP) adjusts the pressure automatically based on the individual's breathing patterns. Other markets in this industry may include bilevel positive airway pressure (BiPAP) machines, which provide varying levels of pressure during inhalation and exhalation, or ventilators specifically designed for pediatric or neonatal use. These devices help individuals with breathing difficulties maintain proper respiratory function while sleeping.</p></p>
