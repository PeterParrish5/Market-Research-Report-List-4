<p><strong>The Post CMP Cleaning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acid Material</li><li>Alkaline Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Post CMP cleaning materials fall into two market types: acid and alkaline materials. Acid materials are used to dissolve and remove metal oxides and contaminants from the wafer surface, while alkaline materials are used to neutralize and remove any remaining acidic residues. Acid materials typically contain chemicals like hydrochloric acid or sulfuric acid, while alkaline materials contain chemicals like ammonium hydroxide or potassium hydroxide. Both types of materials play a crucial role in ensuring the cleanliness and quality of the wafer surface after CMP processing.</p></p>
