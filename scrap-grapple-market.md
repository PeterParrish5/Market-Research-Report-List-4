<p><p>Scrap grapples come in two main market types: hydraulic grapples and mechanical grapples. Hydraulic grapples use hydraulic pressure to operate, providing efficient and precise control over the grabbing mechanism. They are suitable for heavy-duty operations and offer high lifting capacities. On the other hand, mechanical grapples rely on mechanical power for operation, making them more straightforward and cost-effective. They are commonly used for lighter tasks and are popular for their simplicity and reliability. Both types of grapples have their unique advantages and applications in the scrap industry.</p></p>
