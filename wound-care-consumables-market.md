<p><p>Wound care consumables market includes various types such as hydrocolloids, alginates, hydrogels, and others. Hydrocolloids are dressings that contain gel-forming agents to help maintain a moist wound environment. Alginates are made from seaweed and provide a high level of absorption for wounds with heavy exudate. Hydrogels are water-based and provide a cooling effect to help with pain relief. Other types of wound care consumables include foams, films, and antimicrobial dressings, each serving different functions in wound healing.</p></p>
