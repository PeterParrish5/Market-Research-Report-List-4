<p><strong>The Buttermilk Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dried Buttermilk Powder</li><li>Atmospheric Roller Dried Buttermilk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buttermilk powder is available in two main types - Spray Dried Buttermilk Powder and Atmospheric Roller Dried Buttermilk Powder. Spray Dried Buttermilk Powder is produced by spraying liquid buttermilk into a hot air stream to quickly dry it into powder form. On the other hand, Atmospheric Roller Dried Buttermilk Powder is made by passing a thin layer of buttermilk over heated rollers to remove moisture and create a fine powder. Both types are popular choices for adding buttermilk flavor to a variety of dishes.</p></p>
