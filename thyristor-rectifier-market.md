<p><p>Thyristor rectifiers are used in both single-phase and three-phase markets. Single-phase thyristor rectifiers are commonly used in residential and small commercial applications, providing a constant DC output from an AC power source. Three-phase thyristor rectifiers, on the other hand, are used in industrial settings where higher power requirements are needed. They convert three-phase AC power into DC power for applications such as electric motor drives, welding equipment, and battery charging systems. Both types of rectifiers play a crucial role in converting and regulating electrical power in various industries.</p></p>
