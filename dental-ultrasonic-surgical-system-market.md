<p><p>Dental Ultrasonic Surgical System market is divided into two types based on frequency: High Frequency and Low Frequency. High Frequency systems operate at frequencies above 20 kHz and are typically used for precise and delicate procedures, such as root canal treatments. Low Frequency systems operate below 20 kHz, providing more power for cutting through tough tissue like bone during procedures like tooth extractions. Both types of systems offer different benefits and cater to specific surgical needs in the dental field.</p></p>
