<p><p>CMOS 3D Image Sensor market types include Backside-illuminated (BSI), BSI Stacked, and Front-illuminated (FI) sensors. BSI sensors offer superior light sensitivity by capturing light from the backside of the sensor, while BSI Stacked sensors integrate multiple layers for improved performance. On the other hand, FI sensors capture light from the front side and are commonly used in entry-level cameras. Each type has its unique advantages and applications, catering to the diverse needs of the imaging industry.</p></p>
