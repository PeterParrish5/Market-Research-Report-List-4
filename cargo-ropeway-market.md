<p><p>Cargo ropeways are a type of transportation system that uses cables and cars to move goods from one point to another. There are two main types of cargo ropeways: reciprocating and circulating. Reciprocating cargo ropeways have cars that move back and forth along a single track, while circulating cargo ropeways have multiple cars that continuously circulate on a looped track. Both types are commonly used in industries such as mining, forestry, and agriculture to transport materials efficiently and safely.</p></p>
