<p><p>Automotive Die Steel Market Types include Cold Work Die Steel and Hot Work Die Steel. On the other hand, Hot Work Die Steel is designed for applications where materials are formed at high temperatures, needing heat resistance and toughness. Both types of die steel play a crucial role in the automotive industry for producing molds, dies, and other tools used in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
