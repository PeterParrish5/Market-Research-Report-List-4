<p><p>Resin bond wheels come in various types such as Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN), Diamond, Silicon Carbide, and Aluminium Oxide. CBN wheels are known for their superior hardness and thermal conductivity, making them suitable for grinding hard materials. Diamond wheels are highly durable and known for their precision grinding abilities. Silicon Carbide wheels are ideal for grinding non-ferrous materials, while Aluminium Oxide wheels are versatile and commonly used for high-speed grinding applications. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for different grinding tasks.</p></p>
