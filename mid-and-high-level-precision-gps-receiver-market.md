<p><strong>The Mid and High level Precision GPS Receiver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Frequency</li><li>Dual Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single frequency precision GPS receivers are less expensive and ideal for basic mapping and navigation applications. They offer accuracy up to a few meters. Dual frequency precision GPS receivers are more advanced and expensive, offering higher accuracy ranging from a few centimeters to decimeters. They are suitable for applications requiring precise position information like surveying, construction, and agriculture. Both types of receivers receive signals from both the L1 and L2 GPS frequencies, but dual frequency receivers can make use of ionospheric corrections for improved accuracy.</p></p>
