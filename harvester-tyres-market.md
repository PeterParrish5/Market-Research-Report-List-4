<p><ul><li>Radial Agriculture Tires</li><li>Bias (Crossply) Agriculture Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Harvester tyres can be classified into two main types - Radial Agriculture Tires and Bias (Crossply) Agriculture Tires. Radial tyres have a ply pattern that allows for more flexibility and better traction, making them suitable for heavy-duty agricultural machinery like harvesters. Bias tyres, on the other hand, have a crisscross ply pattern that provides stability and durability. Each type of tyre has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the harvester and the terrain it will be operating on.</p></p>
