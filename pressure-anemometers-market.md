<p><p>Pressure anemometers are devices that measure wind speed by detecting the pressure exerted by the wind. There are two main types of pressure anemometers: plate anemometers and tube anemometers. Plate anemometers consist of a flat plate that is oriented perpendicular to the wind direction, while tube anemometers use a tube to measure the pressure changes caused by the wind. Both types are commonly used in various industries for monitoring and analyzing wind speeds and directions.</p></p>
