<p><p>Haemostasis devices in the market are mainly categorized into Vascular Closure Devices (VCD) and Compression Devices. Vascular Closure Devices are used to seal the puncture site in the artery after a catheterization procedure, reducing the risk of bleeding and complications. Compression Devices provide external pressure to the puncture site, aiding in the clotting process and preventing bleeding. Both types of devices play a crucial role in achieving haemostasis and promoting faster recovery for patients undergoing invasive procedures.</p></p>
