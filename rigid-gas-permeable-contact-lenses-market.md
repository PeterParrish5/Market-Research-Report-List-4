<p><p>Rigid Gas Permeable Contact Lenses are available in two main market types - corrective lenses and therapeutic lenses. Corrective lenses are designed to improve vision for individuals with refractive errors such as myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, and presbyopia. On the other hand, therapeutic lenses are used for medical purposes, such as treating keratoconus, corneal irregularities, and post-surgical complications. Both types of lenses offer excellent visual acuity, durability, and oxygen permeability, making them a popular choice for many contact lens wearers.</p></p>
