<p><ul><li>Direct-Acting Electromagnetic Valves</li><li>Sub-Step Direct-Acting Electromagnetic Valves</li><li>Pilot-Type Electromagnetic Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-acting electromagnetic valves operate independently of external sources of pressure or flow, making them simple and reliable. Sub-step direct-acting electromagnetic valves offer more precise control by operating using a small step-by-step movement. Pilot-type electromagnetic valves use a small amount of power to control a larger flow, making them suitable for high-flow applications. Each type of electromagnetic valve offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications based on their design and functionality.</p></p>
