<p><p>Fibre-optic gyroscopes are used for measuring angular velocity based on the interference of light waves. The market for fibre-optic gyroscopes can be divided into two types: single-axis gyroscopes measure rotation in one plane, while multi-axis gyroscopes can measure rotation in multiple planes simultaneously. Single-axis gyroscopes are commonly used in applications such as navigation systems in vehicles, while multi-axis gyroscopes are used in aerospace and defense sectors for precise orientation and stabilization of equipment.</p></p>
