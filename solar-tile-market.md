<p><p>Monocrystalline and polycrystalline are two main types of solar tiles available in the market. Monocrystalline solar tiles are made from a single crystal structure, providing higher efficiency and durability. They are more expensive but offer better performance in low light conditions. Polycrystalline solar tiles, on the other hand, are made from multiple crystal structures, making them more affordable but slightly less efficient. They are a popular choice for residential and commercial applications where space and budget constraints apply.</p></p>
