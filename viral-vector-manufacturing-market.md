Adeno-associated viral vectors are known for their safety and long-term gene expression. Retroviral vectors integrate genetic material into the host genome, making them useful for long-lasting gene therapy. Lentiviral vectors are popular for delivering genes into dividing and non-dividing cells. Other vectors in the market include herpes simplex vectors and vaccinia vectors.</p></p>
