<p><p>Railway friction material market consists of various types such as brake blocks, brake pads, brake shoes, and others. Brake blocks are rectangular pieces installed on the wheelsets to provide friction and stop the train. Brake pads are curved components attached to the caliper for braking purposes. Brake shoes are metal plates with friction material attached to them, used in older train braking systems. Other types of friction materials may include discs, clutches, and anti-slip materials for railways.</p></p>
