<p><p>Cadmium zinc telluride radiation detectors come in various types, with the most common being planar detectors and hemispherical detectors. Planar detectors are flat and offer a large sensitive area, making them suitable for high-resolution imaging applications. Hemispherical detectors have a curved shape, providing 3D imaging capabilities and improved efficiency in detecting low-energy radiation. Other types of detectors include pixelated and coplanar detectors, each offering unique features for specific radiation detection needs.</p></p>
