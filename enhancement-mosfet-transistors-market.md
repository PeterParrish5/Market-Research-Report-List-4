<p><p>Enhancement MOSFET transistors are classified into two main types: N-type and P-type. N-type MOSFET transistors have a channel made of n-type semiconductor material, while P-type MOSFET transistors have a channel made of p-type semiconductor material. N-type transistors are known for their higher electron mobility and faster switching speeds, making them ideal for high-performance applications. P-type transistors, on the other hand, have lower electron mobility but are more commonly used in low-power applications due to their lower leakage current. Both types play key roles in the electronics industry.</p></p>
