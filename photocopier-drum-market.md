<p><ul><li>Organic Type</li><li>Inorganic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photocopier drums are classified into two main types - organic type and inorganic type. Organic drums are made from materials like selenium or organic photoconductors and are known for their high image quality and durability. Inorganic drums, on the other hand, are made from materials like amorphous silicon or cadmium sulfide and offer greater cost-effectiveness and efficiency. Both types cater to different market segments based on their unique characteristics and benefits, catering to specific needs and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
