<p><p>Horizontal Metal Machining Centers are categorized based on the number of axes they utilize in their operations. In the 3-axis market, machines can move in three directions - x, y, and z, making them suitable for simpler machining tasks. The 4-axis market adds a rotary axis to allow for more complex and precise positioning of the workpiece. The 5-axis market further enhances precision and flexibility by adding two additional rotary axes, enabling machining from multiple angles without the need for repositioning.</p></p>
