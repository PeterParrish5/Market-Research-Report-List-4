<p><p>A soil penetrometer is a device used to measure the resistance of soil to penetration or compaction. This information can be used to determine the soil's compaction, moisture content, and other physical properties.</p><p>The soil penetrometer market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
