<p><p>Whole Genome Amplification (WGA) is a technique used in molecular biology to amplify the entire genome of an organism. This process allows for the generation of large amounts of DNA from very small samples, making it a valuable tool in various applications such as genetic research, diagnostics, and forensic analysis. WGA is particularly useful when the amount of available DNA is limited, as it enables researchers to amplify and analyze the entire genome from a single cell or small tissue sample.</p><p>The Whole Genome Amplification Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 1.84% during the forecast period.
