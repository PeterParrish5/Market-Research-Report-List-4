<p><ul><li>StudLink</li><li>StudlessLink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine anchor chain market consists of two main types: StudLink and StudlessLink chains. StudLink chains are characterized by the presence of studs or attachments on every link, providing higher strength and holding power. On the other hand, StudlessLink chains have a more streamlined design without studs, offering lower weight and easier handling. Both types are used in various marine applications, with StudLink chains commonly preferred for heavy-duty anchoring operations, while StudlessLink chains are often used for mooring purposes.</p></p>
