<p><p>A hydraulic paper cutter is primarily of two types: household paper cutter and industrial paper cutter. Household paper cutters are smaller, less powerful machines used for occasional cutting needs at home or in small offices. Industrial paper cutters are larger, more heavy-duty machines designed for frequent and high-volume cutting in commercial printing facilities, binderies, and large offices. Both types of paper cutters operate using hydraulic power, providing a precise and efficient cutting process for various paper materials.</p></p>
