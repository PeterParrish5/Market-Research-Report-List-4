<p><strong>The Waterjet Cutter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterjet cutter market includes two main types: pressurized water and abrasive mixture. Pressurized water cutters utilize highly pressurized water stream to cut through materials like rubber, foam, textiles, and soft metals. On the other hand, abrasive mixture cutters combine water with abrasive materials like garnet to cut through harder materials such as steel, granite, and glass. Both types offer precise and efficient cutting solutions for various industries including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
