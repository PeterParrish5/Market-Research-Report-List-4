<p><strong>The Conventional Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Lenses</li><li>Hard Lenses</li><li>Rigid Gas Permeable Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conventional contact lenses market consists of three main types: soft lenses, hard lenses, and rigid gas permeable lenses. Soft lenses are the most commonly used type, made of flexible plastic that allows oxygen to pass through to the cornea. Hard lenses are made of a rigid plastic material that holds its shape well on the eye. Rigid gas permeable lenses are a newer type that combine the durability of hard lenses with the oxygen permeability of soft lenses, providing clear vision and comfort.</p></p>
