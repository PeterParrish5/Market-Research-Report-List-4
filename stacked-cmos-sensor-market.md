<p><p>Stacked CMOS sensors come in two main types - 2-layer stacked and 3-layer stacked. In a 2-layer stacked sensor, the pixel array and the image processing circuitry are stacked on top of each other, allowing for increased sensor performance and reduced size. On the other hand, a 3-layer stacked sensor adds an additional layer for memory integration, enabling faster data processing and improved overall camera performance. Both types of stacked CMOS sensors offer advancements in image quality and functionality for consumer electronics and professional photography applications.</p></p>
