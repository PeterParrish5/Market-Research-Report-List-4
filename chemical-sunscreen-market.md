<p><p>Chemical sunscreen market can be categorized into two main types: UVA sunscreen and UVB sunscreen. UVA sunscreen primarily protects against long-wave ultraviolet A rays, which are responsible for causing skin aging and wrinkles. On the other hand, UVB sunscreen shields the skin from short-wave ultraviolet B rays, which are known to cause sunburn and contribute to the development of skin cancer. Consumers can choose from a variety of chemical sunscreens that offer protection against either UVA, UVB, or both types of ultraviolet rays.</p></p>
