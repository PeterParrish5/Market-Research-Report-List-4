<p><p>Safety light curtains are available with various output types to suit different applications. PNP output light curtains are used in applications where the load is connected between the sensor output and the positive power supply. NPN output light curtains are suitable for applications where the load is connected between the sensor output and the negative power supply. OSE output light curtains are designed for applications requiring a solid-state output with an external circuit for power supply. Each type caters to specific needs within the safety light curtain market.</p></p>
