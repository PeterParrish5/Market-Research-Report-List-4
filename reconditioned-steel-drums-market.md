<p><p>Reconditioned steel drums come in two main types - tight head and open head. Tight head reconditioned steel drums have a fixed top with a small opening for filling and dispensing liquids. They are commonly used for storing liquids that need to be kept securely sealed. Open head reconditioned steel drums have a removable top with a larger opening, making them ideal for storing and transporting solid materials or liquids that require frequent access. Both types offer cost-effective and environmentally friendly packaging solutions.</p></p>
