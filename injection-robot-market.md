<p><p>Injection robot market includes different types such as servo robot and pneumatic robot. Servo robots utilize electric motors to provide precise and efficient control of movement, making them ideal for high-speed and precision applications. On the other hand, pneumatic robots use compressed air to power their movements, making them suitable for applications requiring lower cost and simpler operation. Both types of robots offer unique advantages and cater to different needs in the injection molding industry.</p></p>
