<p><p>Time of Flight Camera for Smartphone market is divided into two types: CMOS Time-of-Flight Camera and CCD Time-of-Flight Camera. CMOS Time-of-Flight Camera uses a complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor sensor to measure the time it takes for light to bounce back from objects in the scene, providing depth information for 3D imaging and augmented reality applications. On the other hand, CCD Time-of-Flight Camera uses a charge-coupled device sensor for similar depth sensing capabilities. Both technologies are gaining popularity in the smartphone market for their advanced imaging capabilities.</p></p>
