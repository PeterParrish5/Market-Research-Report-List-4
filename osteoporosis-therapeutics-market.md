<p><p>Osteoporosis therapeutics market encompasses various types of medications, including bisphosphonates, hormones, strontium ranelate, raloxifene, denosumab, and others. Bisphosphonates are commonly prescribed to prevent bone loss, while hormones such as estrogen can help maintain bone density. Strontium ranelate is used to increase bone formation and decrease bone resorption. Raloxifene is a selective estrogen receptor modulator, which can help prevent bone loss in postmenopausal women. Denosumab is a monoclonal antibody that inhibits bone resorption. Other medications may include calcitonin or parathyroid hormone therapy.</p></p>
