<p><p>Automotive Op Amps come in three main types: Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Four Channel. Single Channel Op Amps have only one operational amplifier in a single package, making them suitable for simple applications. Dual Channel Op Amps have two operational amplifiers in one package, offering flexibility for more complex circuits. Four Channel Op Amps contain four operational amplifiers in one package, providing even more versatility for advanced automotive electronics systems. Each type caters to different needs and requirements within the automotive industry.</p></p>
