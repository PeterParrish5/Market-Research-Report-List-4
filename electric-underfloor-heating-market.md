<p><p>Electric underfloor heating can be classified into three main types: heating cable, electrothermal, and carbon crystal. Heating cable systems consist of cables that are laid out to provide heat evenly across the floor. Electrothermal systems use a conductive polymer film to generate heat efficiently. Carbon crystal systems utilize infra-red technology to heat the floor surface. Each of these types of electric underfloor heating offers different advantages in terms of comfort, energy efficiency, and ease of installation.</p></p>
