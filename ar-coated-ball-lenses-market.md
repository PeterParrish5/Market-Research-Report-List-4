<p><strong>The AR Coated Ball Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer Coating</li><li>Multilayer Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AR Coated Ball Lenses are available in two main types: Single Layer Coating and Multilayer Coating. Single Layer Coating provides a single anti-reflective coating on the surface of the ball lens, reducing reflection and increasing light transmission. On the other hand, Multilayer Coating consists of multiple layers of coatings, each designed to minimize reflections at different wavelengths, resulting in improved performance across a wider range of wavelengths. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of ball lenses in various applications.</p></p>
