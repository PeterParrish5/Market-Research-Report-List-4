<p><p>Hearing instruments market is segmented into four major types based on their placement: Behind-The-Ear (BTE), In-The-Ear (ITE), In-The-Canal (ITC), and Completely-In-Canal (CIC). BTE devices are worn behind the ear and are suitable for all types of hearing loss. ITE devices fit inside the ear and are more discreet. ITC devices are smaller and less visible than ITE devices. CIC devices are the smallest and most discreet, fitting completely inside the ear canal. Each type caters to different preferences and hearing needs.</p></p>
