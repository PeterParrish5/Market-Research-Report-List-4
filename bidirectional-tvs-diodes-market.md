<p><p>Bidirectional TVS diodes are a type of transient voltage suppression device that can protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes in both directions. There are two main types of bidirectional TVS diodes on the market: axial and radial. Axial bidirectional TVS diodes are cylindrical in shape and are typically used in applications where space is limited. Radial bidirectional TVS diodes are disc-shaped and provide protection against voltage transients in a wide range of electronic devices. Both types offer efficient and reliable protection against voltage surges.</p></p>
