<p><strong>The Automotive Exhausts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li><li>Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF)</li><li>Lean Nox Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust market comprises various types of emission control systems, including Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC), Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF), Lean Nox Trap (LNT), and Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR). DOCs convert harmful gases like carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons into less harmful substances. DPFs trap and remove particulate matter from diesel exhaust. LNTs reduce nitrogen oxide emissions by absorbing and storing them, while SCR systems use a catalyst to convert nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water vapor.</p></p>
