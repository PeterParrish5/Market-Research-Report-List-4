<p><p>Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) cameras are used in two main market types: Area Scan and Line Scan. Area Scan cameras capture images by scanning an entire area at once, making them ideal for capturing still images or scenes. Line Scan cameras, on the other hand, capture images line by line, making them suitable for applications that involve moving objects or continuous inspection processes. Both market types cater to different needs and industries, providing versatile solutions for various imaging requirements.</p></p>
