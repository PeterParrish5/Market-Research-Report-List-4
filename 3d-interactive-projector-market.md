<p><strong>The 3D Interactive Projector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li><li>LCoS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DLP, LCD, and LCoS are the three main types of technologies used in 3D interactive projectors. DLP (Digital Light Processing) projectors use tiny mirrors to reflect light and create an image, resulting in high-quality visuals. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) projectors produce images by passing light through LCD panels, offering vibrant colors and sharp details. LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) projectors use liquid crystals on a reflective silicon surface to create images, providing high resolution and accurate color reproduction. Each technology has its own strengths, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
