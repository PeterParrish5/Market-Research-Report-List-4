This is useful for applications like error detection and confirmation. Magnitude Comparators, on the other hand, compare the numerical value of two input signals to determine which is greater. This is commonly used in applications like numeric data processing and control systems. Both types of comparators play a crucial role in various electronic systems for accurate decision-making and signal processing.</p></p>
