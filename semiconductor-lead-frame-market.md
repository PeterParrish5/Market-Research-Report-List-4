<p><p>Semiconductor Lead Frame market consists of two main types: Stamping Process Lead Frame and Etching Process Lead Frame. Stamping Process Lead Frame involves cutting and shaping metal sheets using a stamping machine to create the lead frame. On the other hand, Etching Process Lead Frame utilizes a chemical process to selectively remove layers of metal to form the lead frame. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by providing a platform for mounting and connecting semiconductor components.</p></p>
