<p><p>Offshore weather services offer three main market types for forecasting: short-range, medium-range, and long-range. Short-range forecasting involves predicting weather conditions within the next few hours to a few days. Medium-range forecasting extends this to around 7-10 days in advance. Long-range forecasting looks even further ahead, typically from 10 days up to several months. Each market type caters to different users and industries, providing crucial information for planning and decision-making in offshore operations.</p></p>
