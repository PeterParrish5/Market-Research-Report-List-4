<p><p>Vertical metal machining centers are classified based on the number of axes they operate on; 3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis. In a 3-axis machine, the cutting tool moves along three axes - X, Y, and Z. A 4-axis machine adds rotation capability around one of these axes. A 5-axis machine offers the most complex movement, allowing cutting on five axes simultaneously. Each type caters to different levels of complexity and precision in metal machining processes.</p></p>
