There are two main types of catalysts used in this market: Reactive Amine Catalysts and Non-reactive Amine Catalysts. Reactive Amine Catalysts help in promoting the reaction between polyols and isocyanates to produce polyurethane foam, while Non-reactive Amine Catalysts act as stabilizers and antioxidants. Both types of catalysts play a crucial role in the production of flexible polyurethane foam, which is used in various industries such as furniture, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
