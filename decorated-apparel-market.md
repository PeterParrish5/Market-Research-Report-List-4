<p><p>Decorated apparel market types include embroidery, screen printing, dye sublimation, direct to garment printing, and other methods. Embroidery involves stitching a design onto fabric, giving a textured and professional appearance. Screen printing uses a stencil to transfer ink onto fabric, creating vibrant colors and detailed designs. Dye sublimation involves transferring dye onto fabric using heat, resulting in vibrant and long-lasting prints. Direct to garment printing allows for intricate designs to be printed directly onto fabric. Other methods may include heat transfer, vinyl cutting, and rhinestone application.</p></p>
