<p><p>Microwave dielectric materials are categorized based on their dielectric constant values. Low dielectric constant materials have values below 10, offering good insulation and transmission properties. Middle dielectric constant materials have values between 10 to 100, balancing between insulation and signal propagation. High dielectric constant materials have values above 100, providing strong signal propagation capabilities. Each type of material caters to different industry needs, ranging from telecommunications to medical devices, based on their unique dielectric properties.</p></p>
