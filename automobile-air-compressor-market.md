<p><p>Automobile air compressors can be classified into two main types: oil-free air compressors and oil-lubricated air compressors. Oil-free air compressors do not require any lubrication and are typically used in applications where oil contamination is a concern. On the other hand, oil-lubricated air compressors use oil to lubricate the internal components for smoother operation and longer lifespan. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
