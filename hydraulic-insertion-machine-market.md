<p><p>Hydraulic insertion machines come in three main types based on their level of automation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require human intervention for every step of the insertion process, offering the most control but also the slowest operation. Semi-automatic machines combine manual control with some automation, increasing efficiency while still requiring human assistance. Fully automatic machines operate independently once set up, offering the highest speed and efficiency but with less flexibility for customization. Each type of machine caters to different production needs and operator preferences.</p></p>
