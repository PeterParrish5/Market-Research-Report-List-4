<p><p>Pre-Clinical Imaging System market can be categorized into standalone imaging systems and multimodal imaging systems. Standalone imaging systems are dedicated to a single imaging modality, such as MRI, PET, or CT, providing high-resolution images for specific research applications. On the other hand, multimodal imaging systems combine multiple imaging modalities, allowing researchers to obtain complementary information and improve overall image quality. These systems are versatile and offer a more comprehensive approach to pre-clinical imaging research, making them increasingly popular in the market.</p></p>
