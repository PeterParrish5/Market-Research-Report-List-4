<p><p>Acoustic sandwich panels are versatile construction materials that offer soundproofing and insulation benefits. There are three main types of acoustic sandwich panels based on their core material: foam core, rubber core, and others. Foam core panels are lightweight and provide good acoustic performance. Rubber core panels offer high impact resistance and vibration damping properties. The category of "others" includes panels with different core materials like rockwool or fiberglass. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of sound absorption, insulation, and durability.</p></p>
