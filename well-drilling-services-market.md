<p><ul><li>Impact Drill</li><li>Rotary Drill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Well drilling services market can be classified into two main types: Impact Drill and Rotary Drill. Impact Drill is a type of drilling method that uses a hammering action to break apart rock formations and create a well. On the other hand, Rotary Drill involves the use of a rotating drill bit to bore into the ground and create a well. Both Impact Drill and Rotary Drill are popular methods used in the well drilling services market due to their efficiency and effectiveness in creating wells for various purposes.</p></p>
