<p><p>Vertical steam generators feature a vertical design where the steam tubes are placed vertically within the generator. This type is commonly used in pressurized water reactors. Horizontal steam generators, on the other hand, have a horizontal design with steam tubes arranged horizontally. They are typically found in boiling water reactors. Both types of steam generators play a crucial role in producing the steam needed to generate electricity in nuclear power plants.</p></p>
