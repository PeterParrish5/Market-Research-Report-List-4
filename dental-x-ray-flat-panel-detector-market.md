<p><p>Dental X-Ray flat panel detectors are used in digital radiography for capturing images of teeth and surrounding structures. There are two main types of detectors - direct conversion and indirect conversion. </p><p>Direct conversion detectors convert X-ray photons directly into electrical signals, resulting in high image quality and efficiency. Indirect conversion detectors use a scintillator to convert X-ray photons into light, which is then converted into electrical signals. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications within the dental X-ray market.</p></p>
