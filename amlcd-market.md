<p><p>AMLCD market types refer to different technologies used in manufacturing LCD panels. A-Si (Amorphous Silicon) technology is commonly used for lower-cost panels with lower resolution and slower response times. P-Si (Polysilicon) technology is known for higher resolution and faster refresh rates, making it suitable for high-end displays. Other technologies in the market include LTPS (Low-Temperature Polysilicon) and Oxide, each offering unique advantages in terms of performance and cost. The choice of technology depends on factors such as budget, performance requirements, and application.</p></p>
