<p><p>The Built-In Central Vacuum System market is divided into two main types: Centralized Type and Distributed Type. In the Centralized Type, a single, powerful unit is installed in a central location, typically in the garage or basement, which then connects to various inlet valves throughout the house via a network of pipes. On the other hand, the Distributed Type involves smaller units installed in each room or area, offering more flexibility but requiring more maintenance. Both types offer a convenient and efficient way to clean homes.</p></p>
