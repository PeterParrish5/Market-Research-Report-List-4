<p><p>Beta-lactam antibiotics are a class of antibiotics that contain a beta-lactam ring in their molecular structure. They are widely used to treat a variety of bacterial infections. However, some bacteria have developed resistance to beta-lactam antibiotics through the production of enzymes called beta-lactamases. Beta-lactamase inhibitors are drugs that can be combined with beta-lactam antibiotics to prevent the breakdown of the antibiotic by beta-lactamases, thus enhancing their effectiveness.</p><p>The Beta-lactam and Beta-lactamase Inhibitors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 1.90% during the forecast period.
