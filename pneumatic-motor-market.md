<p><p>Pneumatic motors are categorized into three main types: vane air motors, piston air motors, and gear air motors. Vane air motors utilize rotating vanes inside a rotor to generate motion. Piston air motors feature pistons that move back and forth within cylinders to produce mechanical energy. Gear air motors use rotating gears to create motion. Each type of pneumatic motor has its own unique advantages and applications, making them suitable for a variety of industrial uses.</p></p>
