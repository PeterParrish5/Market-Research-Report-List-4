<p><ul><li>Millimeter Wave Radiometer</li><li>Interferometric Correlator Radiometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airborne Radiometer Market Types consist of Millimeter Wave Radiometer and Interferometric Correlator Radiometer. Millimeter Wave Radiometer measures the intensity of electromagnetic radiation in the millimeter wave range, allowing for accurate monitoring of atmospheric conditions. On the other hand, Interferometric Correlator Radiometer uses a technique called interferometry to measure slight differences in the phase of electromagnetic waves, enabling high-resolution imaging and monitoring of specific targets. Both types of radiometers are essential tools in remote sensing applications for various industries such as weather forecasting, agriculture, and defense.</p></p>
