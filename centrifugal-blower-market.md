<p><p>Centrifugal blowers are categorized into three main types based on their pressure output: High Pressure, Medium Pressure, and Low Pressure. High-pressure blowers are designed to generate strong airflow at high resistance levels, making them ideal for applications requiring significant air pressure. Medium-pressure blowers offer a balance between pressure and airflow, suitable for a wide range of industrial operations. Low-pressure blowers are designed for applications requiring minimal pressure, but higher airflow. Each type of blower serves specific purposes and is chosen based on the required pressure level.</p></p>
