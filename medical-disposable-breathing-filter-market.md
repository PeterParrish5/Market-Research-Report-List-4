<p><p>Medical Disposable Breathing Filters are classified into two main types: Mechanical Filters and Electrostatic Filters. Mechanical filters work by physically trapping particles and contaminants in the filter material, while Electrostatic filters use an electric charge to attract and capture particles. Both types are widely used in medical settings to ensure the purity of inhaled air and prevent the spread of infections. The market for these filters is driven by the increasing demand for respiratory protection in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
