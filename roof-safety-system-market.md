Guardrails are physical barriers that provide a protective edge around the roof perimeter. Fall arrest equipment consists of harnesses, lanyards, and anchors that protect workers in the event of a fall. Sunroof screens are installed over skylights to prevent falls and protect workers from sunlight exposure. Other types of roof safety equipment include safety nets, warning lines, and roof anchors.</p></p>
