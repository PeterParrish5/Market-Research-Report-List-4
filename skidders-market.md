<p><p>Skidders are commonly categorized into two main market types: Crawler Type and Wheel Type. Crawler Type skidders have tracks instead of wheels, providing better traction and performance in rough terrains. They are ideal for logging in wet or steep areas. On the other hand, Wheel Type skidders have traditional wheels, offering faster speeds and easier maneuverability on flat surfaces. They are more commonly used in less challenging terrains such as plantations or construction sites. Both types have their advantages depending on the specific job requirements.</p></p>
