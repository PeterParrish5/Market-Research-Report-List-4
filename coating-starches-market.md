<p><p>Coating starches refer to various types of starches that are used in the coating industry, each with specific properties and applications. Oxidized starch is modified for improved viscosity and adhesion. Cross-linked starch is modified to resist high temperatures and shear forces. Hydroxyalkyl starch is used for its film-forming and water retention properties. Porous starch has a high surface area and is ideal for encapsulating flavors or active ingredients. Each type of coating starch offers unique benefits for different coating applications.</p></p>
