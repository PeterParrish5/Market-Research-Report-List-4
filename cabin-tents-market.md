<p><p>Cabin tents are available in two main market types: 3-season and 4-season. 3-season cabin tents are designed for use in spring, summer, and fall, offering ventilation and protection from mild weather conditions. They are not suitable for harsh winter conditions. On the other hand, 4-season cabin tents are built to withstand extreme weather, including heavy snow and strong winds, making them ideal for winter camping. These tents are usually heavier and more durable than 3-season tents. Both types cater to different camping needs and environments.</p></p>
