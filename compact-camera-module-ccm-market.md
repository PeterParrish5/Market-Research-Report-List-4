<p><p>Compact Camera Module (CCM) market is divided into two main types based on the image sensor technology used - Charged Coupled Device (CCD) and Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS). While CCD sensors are known for their high-quality images and superior low-light performance, CMOS sensors are more cost-effective and energy-efficient. The competition between these two technologies drives innovation and advancements in the CCM market, leading to improved image quality, smaller form factors, and enhanced features in compact camera modules.</p></p>
