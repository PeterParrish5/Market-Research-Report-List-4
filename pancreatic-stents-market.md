<p><p>Pancreatic stents are classified into three main types: straight, wedge, and curved. Straight pancreatic stents are designed for simple straight ducts, while wedge pancreatic stents are shaped like a wedge to ensure better drainage in irregularly shaped ducts. Curved pancreatic stents are used for ducts with sharp angles or curves. Each type of pancreatic stent is specifically designed to address different anatomical variations in pancreatic ducts, providing effective drainage and symptom relief for patients with various pancreatic conditions.</p></p>
