<p><ul><li>Liquid Foam Gaskets</li><li>Thixotropic Foam Gaskets</li><li>Compact Gaskets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Form in Place Gaskets (FIPG) come in three main types - Liquid Foam Gaskets, Thixotropic Foam Gaskets, and Compact Gaskets. Liquid Foam Gaskets are applied as a liquid that expands and cures in place, providing a flexible seal. Thixotropic Foam Gaskets are similar but have a thicker consistency that prevents sagging during application. Compact Gaskets are pre-formed and provide a solid seal without the need for expansion. Each type has its own unique application and benefits in the automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
