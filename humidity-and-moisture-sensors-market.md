<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Humidity and moisture sensors are vital in measuring the amount of moisture in the air. Relative humidity sensors determine the percentage of moisture in the air compared to the maximum amount it can hold at a given temperature. Absolute humidity sensors, on the other hand, measure the actual amount of water vapor present in the air. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as agriculture, HVAC, and meteorology, providing valuable data for optimal performance and decision-making.</p></p>
