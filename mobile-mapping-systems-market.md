<p><strong>The Mobile Mapping Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Mobile Mapping System</li><li>Backpack Mobile Mapping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The direct mobile mapping system involves mounting a sensor directly on a vehicle to capture geospatial data as it moves along a route. This system is ideal for large-scale mapping projects and infrastructure planning. On the other hand, the backpack mobile mapping system consists of a sensor carried by individuals on foot to gather data in areas inaccessible by vehicles. This system is perfect for urban environments, indoor mapping, and areas with narrow or uneven terrain.</p></p>
