Eccrine Chromhidrosis involves colored sweat from the eccrine glands, usually yellow or pink. Pseudochromhidrosis occurs when sweat reacts with substances on the skin, causing it to appear discolored.</p><p>These different types of chromhidrosis require specific treatment approaches tailored to the underlying cause of the condition.</p></p>
