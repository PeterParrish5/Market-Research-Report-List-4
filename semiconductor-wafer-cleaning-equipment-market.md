<p><ul><li>Rotary Wafer Etching System</li><li>Manual Wet Batch System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer cleaning equipment market can be categorized into two main types: rotary wafer etching system and manual wet batch system. Rotary wafer etching systems use a rotating platform to evenly distribute etchants and clean wafers in a continuous process. On the other hand, manual wet batch systems require operators to load and unload wafers into individual chambers for cleaning. Both systems are essential for removing contaminants and residues from semiconductor wafers to ensure optimal performance and yield in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
