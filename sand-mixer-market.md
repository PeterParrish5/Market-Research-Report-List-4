<p><p>Sand mixers are essential equipment in foundries and construction industries for blending sand and additives. There are three main types of sand mixers in the market: roller sand mixers, blade sand mixers, and counter flow sand mixers. Roller sand mixers use rollers to mix the sand, blade sand mixers use blades, and counter flow sand mixers have an opposite flow of sand and air to mix the materials efficiently. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications in the industry.</p></p>
