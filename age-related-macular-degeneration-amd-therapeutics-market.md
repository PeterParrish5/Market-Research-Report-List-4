<p><p>Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD) Therapeutics Market is divided into two main types based on the progression of the disease: Wet AMD and Dry AMD. Wet AMD involves abnormal growth of blood vessels under the retina, leading to rapid vision loss. Treatments for Wet AMD aim to stop the growth of these blood vessels. Dry AMD is characterized by the gradual breakdown of light-sensitive cells in the macula. Therapies for Dry AMD focus on slowing down the progression of the disease and managing symptoms.</p></p>
