<p><p>Cardiac positioners are devices used during cardiac surgeries to position the heart for better access and visibility. There are four main types of cardiac positioners in the market - Negative Pressure Suction, Mechanically Fixed, Minimally Invasive Suction, and Apex Suckers. Negative Pressure Suction positioners use suction to hold the heart in place, while Mechanically Fixed positioners use clamps or straps. Minimally Invasive Suction positioners offer less invasive options, and Apex Suckers are designed specifically for holding the apex of the heart during surgery.</p></p>
