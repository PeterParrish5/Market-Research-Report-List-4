<p><p>Robotic palletizers and de-palletizers come in two main types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic systems are designed to operate without human intervention, performing tasks such as palletizing or de-palletizing with minimal oversight. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems require some level of human interaction to operate efficiently, such as manually loading or unloading pallets. Both types of systems offer efficiency and faster processing speeds, but fully-automatic systems are generally preferred for high-volume operations, while semi-automatic systems are more suitable for smaller-scale facilities.</p></p>
