<p><p>Bain Maries, a type of food warming equipment, come in two main market types: Wet Heat and Dry Heat Bain Maries. Wet Heat Bain Maries are designed to keep food warm using boiling water, creating a humid environment to prevent food from drying out. Dry Heat Bain Maries, on the other hand, use dry heat to maintain food temperature without adding moisture. Both types are commonly used in commercial kitchens and food service establishments to keep food at safe serving temperatures for extended periods.</p></p>
