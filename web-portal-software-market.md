<p><p>Web portal software is available in two main market types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and run on the company's own servers and infrastructure, providing complete control and customization but requiring more maintenance and upfront costs. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of businesses based on their requirements and resources.</p></p>
