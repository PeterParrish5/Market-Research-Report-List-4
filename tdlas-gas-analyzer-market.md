<p><strong>The TDLAS Gas Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In Situ Laser Analyzer</li><li>Extractive Laser Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TDLAS Gas Analyzer market consists of two main types: in situ laser analyzers and extractive laser analyzers. In situ laser analyzers directly measure gases in the process stream, while extractive laser analyzers extract a sample of gas from the process stream for analysis. In situ analyzers are ideal for real-time monitoring and quick response to changing gas levels, while extractive analyzers are better suited for applications where direct contact with the gas stream is not possible or practical.</p></p>
