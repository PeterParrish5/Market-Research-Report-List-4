<p><strong>The Water-based Muds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersible</li><li>Not Dispersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-based muds market can be categorized into two main types: dispersible and not dispersible. Dispersible muds are those that can disperse in water, forming a homogenous mixture. This type is often preferred for its ease of use and environmental friendliness. On the other hand, not dispersible muds do not dissolve in water and instead form a stable suspension. This type is commonly used in drilling operations where a stable mud is required to carry cuttings to the surface.</p></p>
