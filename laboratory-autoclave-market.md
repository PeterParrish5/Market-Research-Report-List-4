<p><p>Laboratory autoclaves are classified into different types based on their sterilization processes. Gravity Displacement Autoclave uses steam to displace air from the chamber. Pre-vacuum Autoclave removes air from the chamber before sterilization. Steam Flush Autoclave is equipped with a steam generator for faster sterilization. Other types of autoclaves may include pass-through autoclaves, dry heat autoclaves, and rapid cycle autoclaves. Each type offers unique features and benefits depending on the specific sterilization requirements of the laboratory.</p></p>
