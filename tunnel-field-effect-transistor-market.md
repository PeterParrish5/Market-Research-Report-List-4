<p><p>Tunnel Field Effect Transistors (TFETs) are categorized into two main types based on the direction of electron tunneling: lateral and vertical. In lateral tunneling TFETs, the tunneling of electrons occurs horizontally through a thin channel. On the other hand, in vertical tunneling TFETs, the tunneling of electrons takes place vertically through a stacked structure. Both types offer unique advantages in terms of performance and efficiency, making them suitable for various applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
