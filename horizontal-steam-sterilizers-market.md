<p><strong>The Horizontal Steam Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>SFPP</li><li>Pre-Vac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal steam sterilizers are commonly categorized into three types based on the method used to remove air from the chamber prior to sterilization. Gravity sterilizers rely on gravity to displace air, SFPP sterilizers use steam flushes to remove air, and Pre-Vac sterilizers use vacuum pumps to pull air from the chamber. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of efficiency, cycle time, and effectiveness in sterilizing different types of loads. The choice of sterilizer type depends on the specific requirements of the facility and the types of items being sterilized.</p></p>
