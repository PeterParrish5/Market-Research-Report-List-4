<p><p>The Artificial Valve Market consists of three main types: Transcatheter Valve, Tissue Valve, and Mechanical Valve. Transcatheter Valve is inserted through a catheter, reducing the need for open-heart surgery. Tissue Valve is made from animal tissue and is preferred for patients who cannot take blood thinners. Mechanical Valve is made from durable materials and may last longer, but requires the patient to take blood thinners for life. Each type of valve has its own benefits and considerations, catering to different patient needs.</p></p>
