<p><p>Liquid epoxy resin blends are divided into two main types based on their viscosity: low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity liquid epoxy resins are more fluid and easily flow into intricate spaces, making them ideal for applications such as coatings and adhesives. On the other hand, high viscosity liquid epoxy resins are thicker and provide enhanced durability and strength, making them suitable for applications where a higher level of mechanical properties is required, such as in structural adhesives and composite materials.</p></p>
