Non-animal Type softgels are made using alternatives to animal-derived gelatin, such as plant-based or synthetic materials. Both types of softgels cater to different consumer preferences, with some preferring animal-derived gelatin for its traditional use in pharmaceuticals, while others opt for non-animal Type softgels for ethical or dietary reasons.</p></p>
