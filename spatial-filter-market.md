<p><strong>The Spatial Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Smoothing Spatial Filter</li><li>Sharpening Spatial Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spatial filters are used in image processing to enhance or suppress certain features within an image. Smoothing spatial filters reduce noise and blur edges by averaging neighboring pixel values. In contrast, sharpening spatial filters enhance edges and fine details by emphasizing the differences between neighboring pixel values. Both types of filters play a crucial role in improving image quality and are essential tools in various applications such as medical imaging, satellite imagery, and digital photography.</p></p>
