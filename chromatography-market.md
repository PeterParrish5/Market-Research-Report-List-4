<p><p>Chromatography market types include Liquid Chromatography (LC), Gas Chromatography (GC), Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC), and Thin Layer Chromatography. Liquid Chromatography separates compounds in a liquid mobile phase, while Gas Chromatography uses a gas mobile phase. Supercritical Fluid Chromatography utilizes supercritical fluids as the mobile phase. Thin Layer Chromatography involves separating compounds on a thin layer of adsorbent material. Each type of chromatography has its own advantages and applications in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental testing.</p></p>
