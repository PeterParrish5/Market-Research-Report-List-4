<p><p>The commercial satellite launch market is divided into three main categories: GEO satellite market, LEO satellite market, and Sun-synchronous satellite market. </p><p>GEO satellites are placed in high orbits around the Earth, providing global coverage but with higher latency. LEO satellites orbit closer to Earth, offering lower latency but requiring a larger constellation for global coverage. Sun-synchronous satellites orbit the Earth at an angle that ensures they pass over any given point at the same local solar time, ideal for Earth observation and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
