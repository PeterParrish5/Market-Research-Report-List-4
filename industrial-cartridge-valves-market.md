<p><strong>The Industrial Cartridge Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-in Cartridge Valve</li><li>Slip-in Cartridge Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Cartridge Valves Market includes two main types - Screw-in Cartridge Valves and Slip-in Cartridge Valves. </p><p>Screw-in Cartridge Valves are designed to be screwed directly into a threaded cavity in the hydraulic manifold, offering compact and efficient fluid power control solutions. </p><p>On the other hand, Slip-in Cartridge Valves are inserted into a machined cavity in the manifold using O-rings for sealing. They are known for their easy installation and maintenance, making them a popular choice in various industrial applications.</p></p>
