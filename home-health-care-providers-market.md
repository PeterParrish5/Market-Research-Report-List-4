<p><p>Home health care providers offer two main types of services - custodial care and skilled nursing care. Custodial care involves assisting with activities of daily living, such as bathing, dressing, and meal preparation. This type of care is typically provided by home health aides or personal care assistants. On the other hand, skilled nursing care involves more specialized medical services, such as wound care, medication management, and other treatments that require the expertise of a registered nurse or therapist. Both types of care play a crucial role in supporting individuals who need assistance to live independently at home.</p></p>
