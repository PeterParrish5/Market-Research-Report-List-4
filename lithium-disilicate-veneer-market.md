<p><p>Lithium disilicate veneers are categorized based on their flexural strength, with three main types: 200-300 MPa, 300-400 MPa, and more than 400 MPa. Veneers with a strength of 200-300 MPa are suitable for moderate stress-bearing areas. Those in the 300-400 MPa range are ideal for areas with higher stress levels. Veneers with a flexural strength exceeding 400 MPa are recommended for extensive stress-bearing applications. Dentists determine the appropriate type of veneer based on the specific requirements of each patient's oral condition.</p></p>
