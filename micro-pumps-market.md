<p><p>Micro pumps are categorized into different types based on their mechanism of operation. Diaphragm micropumps use a flexible diaphragm to create pressure and move fluid. Piston micropumps use a reciprocating piston to displace fluid. Gear micropumps utilize rotating gears to generate fluid flow. Other types of micro pumps include peristaltic pumps, syringe pumps, and vane pumps. Each type of micro pump has its own unique advantages and applications, catering to various industries such as medical, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
