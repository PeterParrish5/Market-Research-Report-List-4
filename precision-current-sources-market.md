<p><ul><li>Unipolar Current Sources</li><li>Bipolar Current Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision current sources are used in various applications and are available in different market types. Unipolar current sources provide a constant current output in one direction only, making them suitable for applications such as sensor excitation or laser diode driving. Bipolar current sources can provide constant current in both positive and negative directions, making them suitable for applications like electronic testing equipment or electroplating. Both types offer precise control over current output, making them essential components in many electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
