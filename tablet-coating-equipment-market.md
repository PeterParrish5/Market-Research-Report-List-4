<p><ul><li>Standard Coating Pan</li><li>Perforated Coating Pan</li><li>Fluidized Bed or Air Suspension Coater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet coating equipment market is diversified and consists of various types including Standard Coating Pan, Perforated Coating Pan, and Fluidized Bed or Air Suspension Coater. Standard Coating Pan involves the coating of tablets in a rotating pan with a spray gun. Perforated Coating Pan uses a perforated drum to achieve a uniform coating on tablets. Fluidized Bed or Air Suspension Coater suspends tablets in a stream of air while spraying the coating solution. Each type offers unique advantages and is tailored to different manufacturing needs.</p></p>
