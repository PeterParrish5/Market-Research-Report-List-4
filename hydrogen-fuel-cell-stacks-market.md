<p><p>Hydrogen fuel cell stacks come in two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled. Air-cooled stacks use air to dissipate heat generated during the fuel cell operation, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, water-cooled stacks use water circulation to cool down the system, offering better efficiency and performance. Both types have their advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the fuel cell system.</p></p>
