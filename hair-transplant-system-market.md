<p><strong>The Hair Transplant System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Strip Surgery (FUSS)</li><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hair transplant systems involve various techniques such as Follicular Unit Strip Surgery (FUSS), Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT), and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUSS involves removing a strip of scalp from the donor area and transplanting it to the recipient area. FUT involves removing a strip of tissue containing hair follicles and transplanting them to the desired area. FUE involves individually extracting hair follicles from the donor area and implanting them in the recipient area. Each technique offers different benefits and considerations for patients seeking hair restoration.</p></p>
