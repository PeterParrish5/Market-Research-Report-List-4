<p><strong>The Anti Crease Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye-Bath Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent</li><li>Wet Processing Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-crease agents can be classified into two main types: dye-bath lubricant/anti-creasing agent and wet processing lubricant/anti-creasing agent. Dye-bath lubricants are used during the dyeing process to prevent the formation of wrinkles on the fabric, ensuring a smooth and even dyeing result. Wet processing lubricants, on the other hand, are used throughout the wet processing of textiles to reduce friction between fibers and machinery, minimizing creases and improving overall fabric quality. Both types play a crucial role in the textile industry to enhance efficiency and quality.</p></p>
