<p><p>Portable Emergency Ventilators come in various types including electronic, mechanical, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. Electronic ventilators are powered by electricity and can be easily programmed for different settings. Mechanical ventilators use a manual or automated mechanism to deliver breaths. Pneumatic ventilators rely on air pressure to deliver breaths. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine electronic controls with pneumatic mechanisms for enhanced performance. Each type of ventilator has its own unique advantages and is used for different emergency situations.</p></p>
