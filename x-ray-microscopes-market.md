<p><strong>The X-Ray Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission X-Ray Microscope</li><li>Scanning X-Ray Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-Ray microscopes are tools used to observe and analyze materials at the nano-scale level. There are two main types of X-Ray microscopes: Transmission X-Ray Microscopes and Scanning X-Ray Microscopes. </p><p>Transmission X-Ray Microscopes use a thin sample that allows X-rays to pass through, producing high-resolution images of the internal structure of the material. </p><p>Scanning X-Ray Microscopes, on the other hand, scan a focused X-ray beam across the surface of the sample to create detailed images of the material's surface topography and internal structure.</p></p>
