<p><ul><li>Mechanical Trenchers</li><li>Jet Trenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine trenching services encompass various market types such as mechanical trenchers and jet trenchers. Mechanical trenchers use rotating blades or chain cutters to excavate seabed material, while jet trenchers utilize high-pressure water jets to create trenches. Both types of trenchers are commonly used in offshore oil and gas exploration, submarine cable laying, and pipeline installation. Mechanical trenchers are preferred for relatively shallower depths, while jet trenchers are more suitable for deeper and harder seabed conditions. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
