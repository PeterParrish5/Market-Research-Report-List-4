<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Preclinical MRI Equipment</li><li>Superconductive Preclinical MRI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Preclinical MRI equipment are used in research labs for imaging small animals to conduct preclinical studies. There are two main types of preclinical MRI equipment: Permanent Magnet Preclinical MRI Equipment and Superconductive Preclinical MRI Equipment. Permanent Magnet equipment use a fixed magnetic field, making them more cost-effective and easier to maintain. Superconductive equipment use superconducting magnets, providing higher resolution imaging but at a higher cost. Researchers choose between these types based on their imaging requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
